1950's

1950

BI founded, as Jewelry Distributors, Inc. Provides diamond engagement rings at discounted prices to GI's returning from the war.

1953

Re-named Business Builders, Inc. Sells jewelry, luggage and sporting goods to businesses, as awards in consumer contests and incentive programs.

1954

Re-named Business Incentives, Inc. Services include trading stamp and consumer continuity incentive programs.

1960's

1960s

Becomes one of the largest U.S. trading stamp companies with redemption centers in the Midwest and Southeast.

1968

Purchases a travel agency and begins offering travel incentive solutions.

1970's

1975

Celebrates 25 years of business!

1970s

Transitions from a consumer incentives focus to a channel incentives solution provider.

1970s

Expands from a Midwestern presence to a national company with sales offices across the United States.
